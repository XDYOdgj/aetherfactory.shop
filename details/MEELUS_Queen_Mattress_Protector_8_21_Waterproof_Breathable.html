MEELUS Queen Mattress Protector

The MEELUS Queen mattress protector is a stylish and reliable choice for protecting your mattress from spills, stains, and everyday wear. With 100% waterproof protection and a high-density TPU backing, it offers a strong defense against moisture while keeping your sleep surface hygienic and clean.

Designed for a comfortable night's rest, this protector is breathable and moisture-absorbing, helping you stay cool and refreshed throughout the night. Its ultra-soft fabric provides a second layer of protection without compromising on feel, making it perfect for creating a safe and cozy sleep environment.

Whether you're at home, staying at a hotel, or renting a space, the MEELUS Queen mattress protector is ideal for families with kids, seniors, or pets. The deep pocket design fits mattresses from 8\" to 21\", and the non-slip edges keep it secure in place, ensuring peace of mind during every use.

Thanks to its easy-machine wash and quick-dry features, maintenance is hassle-free. The double diamond-quilted top adds an extra layer of cushioning for enhanced comfort, making it a great option for both everyday use and traveling.
