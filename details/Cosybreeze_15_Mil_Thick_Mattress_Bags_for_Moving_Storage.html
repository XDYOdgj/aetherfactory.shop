Cosybreeze 1.5 Mil Thick Mattress Storage Bags

Keep your mattress safe during moves, storage, or when preparing for disposal with these tough and reliable storage solutions. Made from thick, clear polyethylene, these bags provide a protective barrier against dirt, moisture, and light scratches, ensuring your mattress remains in top condition from start to finish.

Why Choose Cosybreeze Mattress Bags?

  • Crafted with 1.5 mil thick clear polyethylene for superior protection.
  • Available in twin and full sizes (54 x 87 x 14 in and 78 x 96 x 14 in) to fit a variety of mattress types.
  • No vent-hole design creates a secure, airtight seal to prevent leaks and contamination.
  • Easy to fold and seal with packing tape, making them convenient for quick storage or transport.
  • Ideal for use in storage units, attics, garages, or even for temporary protection during home moves.

Perfect for Every Situation

Whether you're moving to a new home, storing your mattress for a few weeks, or getting rid of an old one, these bags offer a hassle-free, effective way to keep your mattress clean and undamaged. Their seamless construction ensures a snug fit, providing reliable protection without unnecessary bulk.

Designed with both individuals and families in mind, these storage bags are a must-have for anyone wanting to safeguard their investment. With their durable build and easy-to-use design, they make it simple to transport or store your mattress with confidence.
