Experience Ultimate Comfort with CAROMIO’s Heated Mattress Pad

Stay warm throughout the night with a high-performance heated mattress pad that’s designed for cozy, restful sleep. Whether it’s a cold winter night or you simply need an extra boost, this electric mattress pad delivers the warmth and comfort you deserve.

Key Features
  • High-quality coral fleece for superior warmth
  • 5 adjustable heating levels (86°F-112°F)
  • 10-hour auto shut off for safety
  • Deep pocket design for secure fit
  • Dual control for personalized comfort
Why Choose CAROMIO?

This electric mattress pad offers a flexible and customizable solution to help you maintain a comfortable body temperature. The dual control feature allows each partner to have their own preferred heat setting, making it perfect for cold nights or periods of fatigue. The 17FT power cord adds convenience, and the 18 inch deep pocket ensures a snug, secure fit without any slipping. When the controller is detached, it can be used as a regular underblanket, offering versatility across all seasons and sleep needs.

Indulge in Warmth and Cozy Comfort

Crafted with attention to detail, CAROMIO’s heated mattress pad uses premium coral fleece to create a warm and comfortable sleep environment. It maintains a soft and fluffy texture while being resistant to pilling. The quilted top layer keeps the filling evenly distributed for consistent heat, and the advanced ventilation design ensures you stay comfortable and breath easy all night long.
