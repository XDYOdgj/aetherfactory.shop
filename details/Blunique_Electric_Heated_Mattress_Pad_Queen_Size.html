Blunique's Queen Size Electric Heated Mattress Pad

Experience deeper, more restful sleep with Blunique's electric heated mattress pad designed to keep you warm all night long. Crafted with 100% polyester and advanced 3D air layer fabric technology, it offers unmatched comfort and insulation to transform your mattress into a cozy haven.

The unique 3D air layer structure effectively traps warm air, ensuring you stay at the ideal temperature without sacrificing a soft and pliable feel. This makes your mattress not only warmer but also more inviting, promoting better sleep quality and relaxation.

Featuring a convenient side storage pouch, the pad keeps your controller easily accessible, removing the frustration of searching for tangled cords. This small but thoughtful detail enhances usability and ensures your heating experience is seamless.

With a dual control system, you can set different temperatures for each side of the bed, giving you the flexibility to enjoy personalized heating zones. This is especially great for couples or individuals who have different temperature preferences during sleep.

The pad is equipped with a 10-hour auto shut-off function, providing security and peace of mind. Whether you're preparing for a cold winter night or want an extra layer of comfort, you can rest easy knowing your safety is always prioritized.

Additionally, its machine-washable design makes cleaning and maintenance effortless, while the eco-friendly construction helps you save on energy costs. This is the perfect addition to any bedroom, offering both comfort and efficiency.
